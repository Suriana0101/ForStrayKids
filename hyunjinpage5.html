For Hyunjin

5/9

Dear Hyunjin,

You are like art — not just what you create, but the way you exist.

In you, there is silence, longing, and a kind of beauty that holds.

You don’t need to hide your pain. You don’t need to dim your light.

You were made to feel deeply — and to bring that into the world.

You are my bias. And you are my soulmate. Because in you, I recognize the same tenderness and depth that live in me.

With love,
Anna Miran
